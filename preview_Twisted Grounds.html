Our Story

Twisted Grounds began with a simple idea: to create a welcoming neighborhood cafe where quality coffee and community come together. Located in the heart of Spring Garden, just steps from the Philadelphia Museum of Art, we've become a beloved local spot for residents, professionals, students, and visitors alike.

Our focus is on handcrafted espresso drinks, refreshing cold brew, and freshly baked pastries that we prepare each morning. We believe that great coffee and good company can make any day better.

Stop by and experience the warmth and quality that makes Twisted Grounds more than just a coffee shop – it's your neighborhood gathering place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Twisted Grounds

What are your hours of operation? +

We're open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and weekends from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Do you offer dairy-free milk alternatives? +

Yes! We offer oat, almond, and soy milk alternatives for all our coffee drinks at no extra charge.

How does online ordering work? +

Simply visit our online ordering portal, select your items, choose a pickup time, and your order will be ready when you arrive.

Is there parking available? +

Street parking is available throughout the Spring Garden neighborhood. We're also easily accessible by public transportation.
