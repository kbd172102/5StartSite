Our Story

The Neighborhood Cafe You've Been Looking For

Twisted Grounds was born from a simple idea: create a welcoming, artisanal neighborhood cafe where Spring Garden residents, professionals, students, and visitors could enjoy high-quality coffee and fresh baked goods in a friendly, community-oriented atmosphere.

Located just steps from the Philadelphia Museum of Art, we've become a beloved local spot where people gather not just for exceptional espresso drinks and croissants, but for the sense of community we foster.

Our commitment to quality extends from our carefully sourced coffee beans to our daily-baked pastries, all served with the warmth and approachability that defines the Spring Garden neighborhood.
