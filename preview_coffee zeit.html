Our Story in Old City

A Design-forward Neighborhood Café

Founded in 2018, Coffee Zeit emerged from a simple idea: create a serene space in historic Old City Philadelphia where coffee craftsmanship meets minimalist design.

We believe that the coffee experience should be both exceptional and calming. Our space is designed to be an oasis from the bustling city—a place where every detail, from the ceramic ware to the natural wood surfaces, contributes to a moment of peace.

Our commitment extends beyond the cup. We're proud to be part of the Old City community, hosting workshops and events that foster connection and shared learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your matcha special?

+

We use ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from Japan and prepare it traditionally. Our signature pistachio milk matcha features house-made pistachio milk that creates a uniquely creamy, nutty complement to the matcha's umami notes.

Do you offer dairy alternatives?

+

Yes, we offer oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, and our signature house-made pistachio milk for an additional charge. Our pistachio milk is specially crafted to complement both coffee and matcha beverages.

How can I join a workshop?

+

Workshop schedules are posted monthly on our Instagram and announced to our newsletter subscribers. Spaces are limited to ensure quality instruction, so we recommend signing up early when announcements are made.

Is there seating available?

+

Our minimalist design includes comfortable seating for about 20 guests. We have communal tables, individual workstations, and a cozy corner with armchairs. During peak hours, seating is first-come, first-served.
