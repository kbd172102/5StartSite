39 Years of Trusted Service

Since 1985, Wayne's Garage has been serving the Philadelphia community with honest, reliable auto repair services. As a family-owned business, we've built our reputation on trust, quality workmanship, and personalized customer care.

Our team of ASE-certified technicians brings over 110 years of combined experience to every repair. We're proud to be an AAA-Approved Repair Facility and CARFAX Top-Rated Service Center with a perfect 5-star rating.

Located conveniently at 4521 Springfield Ave, we're easily accessible by public transit and offer a comfortable waiting area for our customers. We stand behind our work with a 2-year/24,000-mile nationwide warranty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation? +

We're open Monday through Friday from 7:30 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays.

Do you offer warranties on your repairs? +

Yes! We stand behind all our work with a 2-year/24,000-mile nationwide warranty for your peace of mind.

How long does a state inspection take? +

Most state inspections are completed within 45-60 minutes. We offer wait-in-your-car service for this quick procedure.

Do I need an appointment for service? +

While appointments are recommended to ensure prompt service, we welcome walk-ins based on availability.

What forms of payment do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and offer financing options for larger repairs through approved lenders.
