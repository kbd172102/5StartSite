Our Story

Born from a love of craft and community.

Nestled on Race Street in Philadelphia's storied Old City neighborhood, Coffee Zeit was born from a simple conviction: that a great café should feel like a breath of fresh air.

We obsess over the details — the origin of our beans, the temperature of our water, the texture of our milk — so that every visit feels effortless and every cup feels like it was made just for you. Our minimalist space is a deliberate counterpoint to the noise of the city outside.

Quality Without Compromise
We source specialty-grade ingredients and never cut corners on preparation, because you deserve the best version of every drink.
Design as Hospitality
Our space is thoughtfully designed to make you feel calm the moment you walk in. Good design is a form of welcome.
Community at the Core
From neighborhood regulars to first-time visitors, everyone belongs here. Our workshops and events are an extension of that belief.

Good questions deserve

We're located at 246 Race Street, Philadelphia, PA 19106 in the heart of historic Old City. We're easily accessible by public transit and there is street parking available nearby. Look for us between 2nd and 3rd Streets.
We're open seven days a week. Our typical hours are Monday–Friday 7am–5pm and Saturday–Sunday 8am–5pm. Hours may vary on holidays — follow us on Instagram for the most up-to-date information.
We use ceremonial-grade matcha and prepare each drink with care and precision. Our signature pistachio milk matcha has become a beloved staple — it combines the earthy depth of high-quality matcha with the creamy, nutty richness of pistachio milk. It's a drink you won't find anywhere else in Philadelphia.
