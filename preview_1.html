Our Story

Twisted Grounds was born from a passion for bringing exceptional coffee and fresh baked goods to the Spring Garden neighborhood of Philadelphia. Located just steps from the iconic Philadelphia Museum of Art, we've become a beloved gathering spot for locals, professionals, students, and visitors alike.

Our commitment to quality is evident in every cup of coffee we brew and every pastry we bake. We believe that great coffee should be accessible to everyone, served in a warm, friendly environment where community connections flourish.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning espresso, grabbing a croissant on your way to work, or settling in for an afternoon of people-watching, we're here to make your experience memorable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation? +

We're open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday through Sunday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Do you offer dairy-free milk alternatives? +

Yes, we offer almond, oat, and soy milk alternatives for our coffee beverages at no extra charge.

Is there seating available inside the cafe? +

Yes, we have comfortable indoor seating for customers who wish to enjoy their coffee and pastries in our cozy cafe atmosphere.

How far in advance should I place an online order? +

We recommend placing your online order at least 15 minutes before you plan to pick up to ensure your items are ready when you arrive.

Are your pastries baked fresh daily? +

Absolutely! All our pastries, including our signature croissants, are baked fresh each morning to ensure the highest quality and freshness.
