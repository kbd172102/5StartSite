Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my repair take? +

Repair times vary depending on the extent of damage. Minor repairs may take 2-3 days, while major collision repairs can take 1-2 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial assessment.

Do you work with insurance companies? +

Yes, we work with all major insurance providers. We can handle your claim directly with your insurance company to make the process as seamless as possible for you.

What is your warranty policy? +

We offer a lifetime warranty on all repairs for as long as you own your vehicle. This covers both parts and labor, giving you peace of mind in our workmanship.

Do you provide rental cars? +

We can help arrange rental vehicles through your insurance coverage. Many policies include rental reimbursement, and we work with local rental agencies to facilitate this service.
