Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles do you service?
We service all makes and models, from modern vehicles to classic cars. Our technicians are trained to work on domestic and import vehicles alike.
Do you offer warranties on your repairs?
Yes, we stand behind all our work with a comprehensive warranty. The specific terms depend on the type of repair and parts used.
How long do most repairs take?
Repair times vary depending on the service needed. Simple maintenance can often be completed same-day, while complex repairs may take longer. We'll provide an estimated timeline when you bring your vehicle in.
