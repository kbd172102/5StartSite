Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Shot Tower Coffee different from other coffee shops?

As both a roaster and coffee shop, we control the entire process from bean selection to brewing. This allows us to ensure exceptional quality at every step and offer unique, freshly roasted beans you won't find elsewhere.

Do you sell coffee beans for home brewing?

Yes! We offer a selection of our freshly roasted coffee beans for purchase. Our team can help you choose the right beans for your preferred brewing method and provide brewing tips.

What types of coffee drinks do you serve?

We serve a full range of espresso-based drinks (espresso, americano, latte, cappuccino, etc.), pour-overs, cold brew, and traditional brewed coffee. All drinks are prepared with our specially roasted beans.

Are your coffee beans sourced ethically?

Absolutely. We partner with ethical growers who practice sustainable farming and pay fair prices. We believe great coffee should be good for everyone in the supply chain.
