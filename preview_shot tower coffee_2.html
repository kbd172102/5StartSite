Our Story

Shot Tower Coffee was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a commitment to craftsmanship. Our name pays homage to the precision and artistry of pulling the perfect espresso shot.

We believe that great coffee starts with great beans, which is why we're dedicated to sourcing the finest quality coffee from sustainable farms around the world. Our small-batch roasting process ensures that each bean reaches its full potential.

More than just a coffee shop, we're a community hub where coffee enthusiasts can gather, learn, and share their love for exceptional coffee.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our coffee and services

Where do you source your coffee beans?

We source our beans from sustainable farms around the world, focusing on regions known for exceptional coffee quality. We prioritize direct trade relationships to ensure fair compensation for farmers and the highest quality beans for our customers.

Do you offer coffee subscriptions?

Yes! We offer flexible subscription options for our freshly roasted beans. Choose your preferred roast profile and delivery frequency to always have fresh coffee on hand.

Can I visit your roasting facility?

We offer scheduled tours of our roasting facility where you can learn about our process and see our equipment in action. Contact us to schedule a visit.

Do you offer barista training?

We offer workshops and training sessions for coffee enthusiasts looking to improve their home brewing skills or learn more about coffee preparation techniques.

Ready to Experience Exceptional Coffee?

Visit Shot Tower Coffee today and discover why craft matters. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso or settling in for a tasting experience, we can't wait to share our passion with you.
