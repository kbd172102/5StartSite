Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your coffee "specialty"?

Specialty coffee refers to the highest grade of coffee beans, scoring 80 points or above on a 100-point scale. We source these premium beans and roast them to highlight their unique flavor profiles, ensuring a superior cup every time.

Do you sell coffee beans for home brewing?

Yes! We offer our freshly roasted coffee beans for purchase. Choose from our current selections, each with distinct flavor notes. Our staff can help you select the perfect beans for your preferred brewing method.

What types of espresso-based drinks do you serve?

We serve a full range of espresso-based drinks including cappuccinos, lattes, macchiatos, flat whites, and of course, perfectly pulled espresso shots. All can be customized with alternative milks and flavor options.

How often do you roast your coffee beans?

We roast multiple times per week in small batches to ensure maximum freshness. This approach allows us to maintain quality control and provide our customers with coffee at its peak flavor.
